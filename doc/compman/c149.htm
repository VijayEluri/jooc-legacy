sourcepath (ooc) vs classpath (Java)

In Java, classes have a 'package org.blah.thingy.feature;' declaration at the beginning of their source files. In ooc, they don't. So you have to be careful with your path: changing your path means potentially changing the package in which ooc thinks your classes are.
